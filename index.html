[ 01 — About ]

One company.
Two worlds.

Metachasm exists at the intersection of the digital and the physical. We design and build bespoke software systems — from mobile apps and PWAs to full enterprise backend architectures — while simultaneously operating a precision fabrication division that brings ideas into the real world through 3D printing and custom manufacturing.

No matter your industry, no matter your domain — healthcare, finance, retail, engineering, entertainment — we build exactly what your challenge requires. No templates. No off-the-shelf constraints. Pure custom.

[ 03 — Process ]

How we work

A battle-tested methodology refined across hundreds of engagements.

01

Discover

Deep-dive into your domain, users, constraints, and goals. We don't assume — we interrogate the problem space until we understand it better than anyone.

02

Design

Architecture decisions, system design, UX flows, and physical CAD modeling — everything mapped before a single line of code or filament is laid.

03

Build

Iterative sprints with continuous client visibility. Both digital and physical builds follow strict quality gates — no surprises at handover.

04

Deploy

Production deployment, physical delivery, documentation, and knowledge transfer. We hand over working systems, not just artifacts.

05

Maintain

Ongoing support, versioning, and evolution. As your needs grow, we grow with you — retainer or on-demand.
